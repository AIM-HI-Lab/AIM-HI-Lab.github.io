About Our Lab

The AIM-HI Lab at Cleveland Clinic's Department of Urology is dedicated to developing and applying artificial intelligence solutions to improve kidney tumor diagnosis and prognosis. Our research focuses on cutting-edge AI technologies for medical imaging analysis and natural language processing to enhance clinical decision-making and patient care in urology.

Research Areas

Kidney Tumor Imaging

Advanced AI algorithms for analyzing kidney tumor imaging to improve cancer detection and prognosis prediction

LLM-Based Chart Review

Automating medical chart review using Large Language Models to extract and analyze clinical information

Clinical Integration

Implementing AI solutions in urological practice to enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning

Recent Publications

Automated Nephrometry Scores Through Direct Prediction of Each Component

Seshadri, R., Siva, J., Bartholomew, A., Goebel, C., Morato, B. L., Wallerstein-King, G., Scovell, J., Wang, B., Campbell, R., Ozery-Flato, M., Barros, V., Gabrani, M., Rosen-Zvi, M., Ward, R., Campbell, S., Abouassaly, R., Remer, E., & Weight, C.

Urologic Oncology: Seminars and Original Investigations, 43(3), 56

2025

This study introduces a novel approach to automate both RENAL and PADUA nephrometry scores by directly predicting each component using deep learning. By eliminating the need for manual segmentation and geometric calculations, this method significantly reduces interobserver variability and improves efficiency in kidney tumor assessment.

Automated RENAL Nephrometry Score Computation Using Deep Learning

Seshadri, R., Siva, J., Bartholomew, A., Goebel, C., Morato, B. L., Wallerstein-King, G., Scovell, J., Wang, B., Campbell, R., Ozery-Flato, M., Barros, V., Gabrani, M., Rosen-Zvi, M., Ward, R., Campbell, S., Abouassaly, R., Remer, E., & Weight, C.

Urologic Oncology: Seminars and Original Investigations, 42(3), 56

2024

This work presents a deep learning-based system for automated computation of RENAL nephrometry scores from CT scans. The system demonstrates high accuracy in predicting tumor complexity, offering a reliable tool for surgical planning and outcome prediction in kidney cancer management.

Automated PADUA Nephrometry Score Computation Using Deep Learning

Seshadri, R., Siva, J., Bartholomew, A., Goebel, C., Morato, B. L., Wallerstein-King, G., Scovell, J., Wang, B., Campbell, R., Ozery-Flato, M., Barros, V., Gabrani, M., Rosen-Zvi, M., Ward, R., Campbell, S., Abouassaly, R., Remer, E., & Weight, C.

Urologic Oncology: Seminars and Original Investigations, 41(3), 56

2023

This study introduces an AI-powered system for automated PADUA nephrometry scoring, addressing the challenges of manual scoring in kidney tumor assessment. The system provides consistent and efficient evaluation of tumor complexity, aiding in treatment planning and risk stratification.
